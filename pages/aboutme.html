Hi there – I'm Travis Bright, a professional psychic medium with a passion for helping people find peace, clarity, and sometimes a little bit of magic along the way. Mediumship isn’t just what I do – it’s who I am. With a strong intuitive gift and a deep respect for spirit, I work with open-minded souls who are seeking healing, answers, or simply a sense of connection beyond the physical world. Whether you're hoping to hear from a loved one or you're navigating life’s mysteries, I offer grounded, heart-centered guidance in a safe and welcoming space. I believe that spiritual work should be meaningful, but that doesn’t mean it has to be heavy all the time. Expect honesty, warmth, and probably a laugh or two – especially if you catch me after a nap or on a lucky streak at bingo. And if you know how to play a good hand of Rook? We might just be instant friends. Thanks for stopping by – I'm truly honored to walk this path with you. Let’s connect when you’re ready.
